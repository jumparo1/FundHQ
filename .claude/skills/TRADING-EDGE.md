# Trading Edge — Jumparo's Playbook

## Trader Profile
- **Style:** Mean Reversion scalps (primary), Momentum shorts, Narrative front-running
- **Timeframe:** 1min scalps (active trading), Daily/Weekly (investing & research)
- **Assets:** Altcoins (crypto primary), occasionally BTC/ETH, expanding into equities
- **Platforms:** Bybit, Hyperliquid (trading), Fund HQ (research)
- **Account size:** $10K-25K range, 1% risk per trade
- **Max daily loss:** 3%, max 3 trades/day
- **Active hours:** Morning session, 3h max

## Core Strategy: MR Scalps (Mean Reversion)
- Fade fast spikes into S/R levels on 1-minute chart
- Entry trigger: Fast spike into level + low volume + choppy PA (at least 2 of 3)
- Entry type: Limit orders
- Stop: Swing low at least 1h old
- Target: 1R minimum
- Hold time: Minutes to 30min
- Best on high-volatility altcoins

## Strategy: Momo Short (100% WR on 7 journal trades)
- Momentum breakdown shorts
- Pattern: Strong downtrend → brief consolidation → continuation breakdown
- Short when RSI/StochRSI both overbought on multiple timeframes
- Wait for first bearish candle close below key level
- SL above recent high, TP at next support

## Strategy: SMT Divergence (Confluence)
- BTC makes higher high but ETH doesn't (or vice versa) = reversal signal
- Used as confluence with other setups, not standalone
- Best on 15m+ timeframes as confirmation

## Indicator Settings
- StochRSI: (3, 3, 14, 14) — OB/OS zones: 20/80
- RSI: 14-period standard
- VWAP: Anchored to session start
- EMA: 21, 50, 200 for trend
- Bollinger Bands: 20, 2.0 for mean reversion
- Volume Profile for key levels
- FVG (Fair Value Gap) as targets
- Order Blocks as S/R zones

## Risk Management (Non-Negotiable)
- 1% risk per trade, never move stop further away
- Max 3-5% total portfolio at risk simultaneously
- Never >3 correlated alts in same direction
- After rule violation: drop to 0.5% risk, 2 trades/day for 5 days
- After liquidation: mandatory 1-week pause
- Max 5x leverage on alts
- If you feel the loss emotionally, you're oversized
