# Asymmetric Opportunity Framework

## What Makes a Setup Asymmetric
An asymmetric bet has **capped downside** and **uncapped or outsized upside**. Risk:reward minimum 1:3, ideally 1:5+.

## The 5-Lens Filter
Every opportunity must pass all 5 lenses:

### 1. Catalyst (Why Now?)
- Protocol upgrade / mainnet launch / token unlock cliff ending
- Regulatory clarity (ETF approval, legal win)
- Partnership / integration with major protocol
- Narrative shift (AI, RWA, restaking cycles)
- **Red flag:** No identifiable near-term catalyst = skip

### 2. Valuation (Is It Cheap?)
- FDV/Revenue < 50x (vs sector median)
- FDV/TVL < 1.5x for DeFi
- MCap/FDV ratio > 0.5 (low future dilution)
- Trading below previous cycle ATH by 80%+ but fundamentals improved
- **Compare:** Always relative to sector peers, never in isolation

### 3. Momentum (Is It Turning?)
- Price reclaiming key EMAs (21, 50, 200 daily)
- Volume increasing on up days
- RSI divergence (price lower low, RSI higher low)
- Breaking out of accumulation range
- **Don't fight the trend** — momentum confirms the thesis

### 4. Risk/Reward (Where's My Stop?)
- Clear invalidation level (structure break, key support)
- Max loss defined before entry (1-2% of portfolio per trade)
- Upside target at least 3x the risk
- Position size based on distance to stop, not conviction
- **If you can't define the stop, skip the trade**

### 5. Narrative (Does the Market Care?)
- Fits a trending narrative (AI, RWA, modular, restaking)
- CT/social sentiment turning positive but not euphoric
- Institutional interest signals (Grayscale, fund allocations)
- Not priced in yet — early innings of narrative adoption
- **Contrarian is good, but too early = wrong**

## Setup Archetypes

### Mean Reversion (MR) Long
- Oversold bounce from key support
- RSI < 30, price at lower Bollinger Band
- Hammer/engulfing candle at support
- Stop below the wick, target at upper range
- **Best in:** Range-bound markets, after capitulation

### Breakout
- Price consolidating at resistance with decreasing volume
- Breakout on 2x+ average volume
- Retest of breakout level = entry
- Stop below breakout level, target = measured move
- **Best in:** Trending markets, after accumulation

### Narrative Front-Run
- Identify upcoming catalyst 2-4 weeks out
- Enter during quiet accumulation phase
- Size small, add on confirmation
- Exit before or at the event (sell the news)
- **Best in:** Bull markets, clear catalyst calendar

### Deep Value Recovery
- Project trading at 90%+ below ATH
- Fundamentals improved since ATH (more users, revenue, TVL)
- Team still building (GitHub activity, updates)
- Low FDV relative to comparable projects
- **Best in:** Bear market bottoms, early recovery

## Anti-Patterns (What to Avoid)
- **Chasing pumps** — if it's already up 50%+ in a week, you missed it
- **Fighting the trend** — shorting in a bull, longing in a bear
- **Illiquid tokens** — can't exit when you need to
- **Token unlock cliffs** — massive supply hitting = sell pressure
- **Team dumping** — check on-chain flows before entry
- **Hype without metrics** — narrative alone isn't enough

## Position Sizing Rules
- Max 2% risk per trade (distance to stop × position size = 2% of portfolio)
- Core positions: 5-10% of portfolio
- Satellite/speculative: 1-3% of portfolio
- Never more than 25% in a single sector
- Cash reserve: always keep 20-30% dry powder
